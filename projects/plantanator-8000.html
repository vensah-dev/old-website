The Plantanator 8000

The Plantanator is an affordable, intuitive, modular and scalable device to grow your plant autonomously and reports live plant status remotely. The Plantanator is designed with the ultimate goal of making homegrown produce and plants accessible to everyone to reduce the carbon emissions from food production and distribution. However, the Plantanator does not stop there as it has have developed a step further to be modular and scalable which allows consumers to use the Plantanator for industrial farming easily at an affordable price. It also enables large scale farming to be done or little to no plots of land as multiple Plantanator devices can be used alongside urban farming techniques or with vertical farming structures.
